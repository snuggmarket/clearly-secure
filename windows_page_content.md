## Our Window Services

### 1. Misted & Broken Glass Replacement
Condensation between double-glazing panes is a sign of a "blown" seal. This doesn't just look bad; it reduces your home's energy efficiency. We provide precision-measured glass replacements that fit perfectly into your existing frames.

### 2. Window Lock & Handle Repairs
Is your window difficult to close or won't lock properly? We repair and replace:
- Espagnolette (Espag) locks
- Friction stay hinges (including fire escape and easy-clean options)
- Cockspur and inline handles
- Key-locking upgrades for enhanced security

### 3. New Double-Glazed Windows
When a repair isn't enough, or if you're looking to modernize your home’s appearance, we offer a range of premium uPVC and Aluminum windows. 
- **Energy Efficient:** A-Rated as standard to keep your home warm and bills low.
- **Custom Styles:** Casement, Tilt & Turn, and Sliding Sash options.
- **Security Focused:** Internal beading and multipoint locking systems.
